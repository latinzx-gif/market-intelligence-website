# The Mor Prom+ Super App Ecosystem

## What Happened
The Thai government is consolidating 50+ health apps into Mor Prom+—a national health "Super App" (SCORED-SIG-111). Governed by the Digital Health Act and AI Act (SCORED-SIG-112), the app synchronizes with IoT home devices to provide proactive wellness. Mohpromt Credits gamify healthy behaviors via financial nudges (SCORED-SIG-114).

## Why It Matters
Thailand is moving from hospital-centric care to a decentralized "Home Care" model. The super app acts as a "Health Command Center," turning personal health data into a liquid asset that can be used for loyalty rewards or personalized medical interventions.
