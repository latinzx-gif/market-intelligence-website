# Biological Plastic Defense

## What Happened
Thailand has transitioned from environmental plastic reduction to personal biological defense (SCORED-SIG-121). With 80% micro-plastic accumulation in specific Thai river basins, a new market for polysaccharide-based bio-filtration (okra/fenugreek) and gut-barrier probiotics has emerged. Simultaneously, the 2027 recycling deadline is driving "Zero-Waste" circular packaging in the supplement sector (SCORED-SIG-130).

## Why It Matters
Environmental health is now a modifiable personal risk. Consumers view micro-plastics as a "silent inflammatory" threat. Protecting the gut barrier from plastic translocation is the new focus for "Eco-Longevity" brands, bridging ESG goals with high-stakes health outcomes.
