# AI-Driven School Nutrition (Digital Nudge)

## What Happened
The Thai School Lunch Platform has evolved into a comprehensive AI-driven nutritionist for 40,000+ schools (SCORED-SIG-107). Simultaneously, there is a regulatory crackdown on HFSS (High Fat, Sugar, Sodium) marketing, including the banning of cartoon characters on packaging (SCORED-SIG-108).

## Why It Matters
The government is utilizing AI to manage the "Double Burden" of malnutrition (obesity vs. stunting). By automating meal planning and tray verification, Thailand is ensuring that the limited educational budget delivers maximum nutritional ROI to prevent early puberty and long-term NCDs.
