# [Public] The Bifurcated Student Market: Acute Shots vs. Daily Baseline

**Topic:** Market Structure 2026
**Reading Time:** 3 Minutes

The Thailand student brain health market is no longer a single category. It has split into two distinct tracks, each with its own pricing, retail, and consumer ritual logic.

### Track 1: The Daily Baseline
This is the foundational nutrition segment dominated by **Multi-pack UHT Milk** (e.g., Hi-Q, Foremost). 
- **Goal:** Long-term DHA and B12 supplementation.
- **Pricing:** Optimized for volume at ~19 THB per serving.
- **Purchase Trigger:** Parent-led grocery shopping.

### Track 2: The Acute Intervention
This is the high-visibility segment of **Concentrated Functional Shots** (e.g., Peptein, Brand's Gen U).
- **Goal:** Immediate focus boost during exam windows.
- **Pricing:** Premium single-serve at ~31 THB.
- **Purchase Trigger:** Student-led impulse buy during high-stress study cycles.

### The Emerging Middle
Our analysis identifies a growing whitespace for **"Hybrid Formats"**—products that offer the performance claims of Track 2 at the multi-pack price point of Track 1. Brands that can bridge this gap will dominate the full 2026 academic lifecycle.
