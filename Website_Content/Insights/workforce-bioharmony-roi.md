# Workforce Bioharmony ROI

## What Happened
Thai corporations are treating executive cortisol levels as a Key Performance Indicator (SCORED-SIG-123). "Work-Life Architecture" platforms use real-time biometrics to trigger automated stress-interventions. Biometric wellness is now a primary retention tool ("Golden Handcuffs") in the shrinking Thai talent pool.

## Why It Matters
Executive burnout is a national economic crisis (7/10 Bangkok employees). Moving from reactive "gym perks" to predictive physiological monitoring allows firms to quantify the ROI of health interventions in terms of talent retention and productivity.
