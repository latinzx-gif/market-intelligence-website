# Nutrient Density over Yield (Regenerative BCG)

## What Happened
Thailand’s agriculture is shifting from volume-based yield to precision nutrient density (SCORED-SIG-122). Wolffia (Watermeal) has emerged as the cornerstone of the BCG future food strategy, showing 20-50% higher flavonoid content in regenerative systems. NECTEC's IoT soil platforms allow smallholders to sell "Nutrient-Verified" produce to premium FMCG players.

## Why It Matters
In a super-aged society, the value of a crop is measured by its biological impact. Regenerative agrotech provides a "Nutrient Premium," where produce retains 80% of its density post-harvest, significantly outperforming conventional crops.
