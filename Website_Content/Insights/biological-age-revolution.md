# The Biological Age Revolution - From Clinical to Retail

## What Happened
Thailand has transitioned from traditional medical tourism to an advanced "Longevity Economy." High-end clinics like VitalLife and Samitivej have standardized DunedinPACE epigenetic testing (SCORED-SIG-101). Simultaneously, mass-market retailers like 7-Eleven and Watsons are expanding into NAD+ precursors (NMN/NR) in accessible gummy and RTD formats (SCORED-SIG-104), supported by streamlined Longevity Visas (MT/DTV) for international age-reversal tourists (SCORED-SIG-113).

## Why It Matters
Biological age—not chronological age—is becoming the primary KPI for health-conscious Thai consumers. This creates a "Healthspan" market where supplements are viewed as biological insurance. The democratization of clinical-grade bioactives (NAD+) into convenience stores signals a massive shift from "niche biohacking" to "mass-market longevity."
