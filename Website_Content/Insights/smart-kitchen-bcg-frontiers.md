# Smart Kitchen BCG Frontiers

## What Happened
Thai smart kitchens are being optimized for "Future Food" integration under the Bio-Circular-Green (BCG) economy model (SCORED-SIG-115). Smart appliances now feature "cloud-based recipes" for plant-based and "immune-boost" proteins, with automated dietary tracking synced to the public health cloud.

The kitchen is the new site for proactive health management. Thailand's "Kitchen of the World" strategy is evolving to include high-value, tech-driven "Functional Proteins" that address specific health biomarkers (e.g., blood pressure, immunity).
