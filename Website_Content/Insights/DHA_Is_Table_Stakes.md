DHA appears widely across kids nutrition, UHT milk, and brain-support adjacent products. In a crowded cognitive nutrition space, DHA can still support credibility, but the stronger strategy is to connect it to a sharper use occasion or emotional need.

For new product positioning, the opportunity is to move beyond generic brain nutrition and define a more specific student job: study readiness, calm focus, recall confidence, or post-study recovery.
