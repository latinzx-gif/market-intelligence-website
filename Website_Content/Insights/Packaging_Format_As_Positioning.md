Packaging does more than contain the product. In functional beverage and kids nutrition categories, it tells the shopper whether the product belongs to daily routine, exam support, premium development, or supplement-like territory.

For innovation work, the key question is not only what the pack looks like. It is what role the pack tells the buyer and user to assign to the product.
