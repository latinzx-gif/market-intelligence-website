# Diagnostic Retail Hubs

## What Happened
Point-of-sale locations in Bangkok (Siam Square) are transforming into bio-intelligent diagnostic hubs (SIG-128). Kiosks now offer 15-minute blood-spot biomarker checks, providing instant "Retail Prescriptions." This shift moves healthcare diagnostics from the hospital to the "Grab-and-Go" daily commute.

## Why It Matters
For Gen Z and professionals, healthcare is an "On-the-go" utility. Instant biomarker validation (Glucose/Cholesterol/Vitamin D) drives immediate, data-verified purchases of functional foods, increasing retail conversion by an estimated 50% compared to unverified browsing.
