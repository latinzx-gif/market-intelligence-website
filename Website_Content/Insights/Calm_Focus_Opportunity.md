The cognitive nutrition category often frames performance as activation: active mind, exam readiness, and brain charge. That leaves room for a softer but potentially powerful proposition: calm focus for study moments.

This territory should be treated as a hypothesis until more consumer evidence is collected from reviews, TikTok comments, interviews, or concept testing. It also requires careful claim-risk review before becoming final product wording.
