# Silver Satiety & Precision Mobility

## What Happened
The Thai senior nutrition market has pivoted to precision medical nutrition (SCORED-SIG-102). Brands like Thai Otsuka and Abbott are utilizing CaHMB and Leucine to fight sarcopenia. Innovation has also reached food texture, with Texture-Modified Foods (TMF) moving from "Mush" to 3D-printed/Molded meals that maintain visual dignity for seniors (SCORED-SIG-103).

## Why It Matters
With Thailand becoming a "super-aged" society, the "Silver Economy" is a $75B opportunity. Seniors are no longer passive care recipients; they are "high-agency consumers" seeking mobility and independence. Functional food that solves the "Dysphagia Barrier" while providing muscle protein synthesis is the new gold standard.
