# DataClaw Intelligence Report #015

## Executive Summary
The Thai functional beverage market has reached a critical inflection point in 2026. The implementation of **Sugar Tax Phase 3** combined with a government-led "Normal Sweetness" nudge has permanently reset the student palate.

This report identifies the transition of sugar from a health concern to a **"Cognitive Disruptor"**. Parents are now actively avoiding sugar in focus products to prevent "Hyper-activity" during study sessions.

## 1. The Palate Nudge: Halving the Sweetness
Major coffee chains now default to **50% sugar** (SIG-075). Students are becoming habituated to "Bitter-Bright" profiles (Matcha) over "Sweet-Berry" profiles.

- **Retail Mandate:** 100% of new functional launches are now zero-sugar, utilizing **Stevia, Erythritol, or Monk Fruit**.
- **The "Hyper" Fear:** Parents explicitly choose sugar-free to prevent symptoms that interfere with evening review sessions (SIG-043).

## 3. Strategic Verdict / Recommendations
1. **Marketing Positioning (Cognitive-Safe):** Re-brand zero-sugar claims. Move from "Weight Management" to **"Focus-Safe"** or **"Anti-Hyper."**
2. **R&D (Natural Molecules):** Invest in **Monk Fruit (Luo Han Guo)** for a "Natural" profile that avoids the bitter aftertaste of Stevia.
