## Executive Summary
The "Tired but Wired" student demographic in Thailand is driving a fundamental shift in the nighttime consumption window. We are observing the transition from reactive insomnia remedies to **"Chrono-Nutritional Recovery"**—a proactive framework that positions nutrition as a tool for a "better tomorrow" through a "smarter tonight."
