# Summary: DataClaw Intelligence Report #005

**Topic:** Functional Night-Time Infusions - Thailand 2026

## 1. The Recovery Gap
A $45M whitespace has emerged in the Thai student market for "High-Performance Recovery." While daytime focus is saturated, the window between 19:00 (Post-study) and 01:00 (Bedtime) is currently underserved by functional beverages that provide "Zen" without insomnia.

## 2. The Savory Pivot
Consumers are moving away from sweet "Relaxation Teas" toward **Savory Botanicals.**
- **Leading Ingredients:** Koji, GABA-rich Miso, Hojicha, and Tart Cherry.
- **Consumer Pain Point:** The "Matcha Dilemma" (Focus vs. 3 AM insomnia).

## 3. Circadian Marketing
The industry is shifting from "Reactive" (Sleep aids for insomnia) to "Proactive" (Circadian stacks for a better tomorrow). 
- **Premium Indicators:** Deep indigo packaging, sugar-free certifications, and "Cellular Repair" claims.

## 4. Strategic Takeaway
"Ownership of the **'Sunset Ritual'** is the next high-value growth pillar. Brands that can bridge the psychological gap between 'Indulgence' and 'Scientific Recovery' will dominate the high-margin premium segment in 2026."
