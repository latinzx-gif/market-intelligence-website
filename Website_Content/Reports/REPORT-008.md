## Executive Summary: The Performance Paradox
As of Q2 2026, the Thai student market has reached a state of "Stimulant Exhaustion." While daytime focus is saturated, the **19:00 - 01:00 study window** remains a critical pain point. Students are reporting a **66.3% prevalence of poor sleep** due to the "Tired but Wired" paradox—the metabolic inability to transition from high-intensity study to restful recovery.

We identify a **$45M market whitespace** for "Chronobiotics"—functional beverages designed to reset the circadian rhythm rather than induce sedation. This report maps the rise of **Savory Botanicals** (Miso, Koji) and the newly approved **Psychobiotic strains** that will define the night-recovery segment in 2027.
