# DataClaw Intelligence Report #019

## Executive Summary
The late-night study window (21:00 - 01:00) has traditionally been dominated by high-sodium instant noodles. However, our 2026 research identifies a fundamental **"Savory Pivot"** among performance-conscious students. We observe a **$60M whitespace** in functional savory snacks and "Drinkable Whole-Foods" that provide satiety without "Noodle Fog."

## 1. The Rise of "Functional Umami"
Umami is being re-positioned as a marker of **"Physiological Calm."**

- **Study Broths:** Artisanal and FMCG leaders are positioning Miso and Chicken-based broths as **"Electrolyte Recovery"**. These provide glutamate precursors for GABA production (SIG-029).
- **Drinkable Whole-Foods:** Winners like **MUST Chicken Protein** create a new category of "Hefty" functional drinks that satisfy hunger while maintaining a clean performance profile.

## 2. Retail Adjacency: The 7-Eleven Shift
- **De-Categorization:** Instant Miso is moving from "Soup" to the **"Functional/Refrigerated"** section in high-traffic student stores.
- **Convenience Innovation:** Shift toward **"Single-Serve Sticks"** that mimic the convenience of noodles but deliver supplement-grade science.

## 3. Strategic Verdict / Recommendations
1. **R&D (The Umami Stack):** Prioritize savory bases (Miso, Shiitake) for nighttime lines. Incorporate **GABA and RiboCARE Sleep** to back the "Recovery" claim.
2. **Positioning (The Noodle-Free Zone):** Directly challenge the instant noodle occasion. Use messaging like **"The Smarter Supper"**.
3. **Visual Aesthetics:** Use earthy minimalist design to communicate **"Natural Recovery"** and metabolic efficiency.
