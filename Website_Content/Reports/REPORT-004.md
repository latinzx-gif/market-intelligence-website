## Executive Summary
- **Texture-First Value:** In 2026, Thai students prioritize the "Chewy-Crunchy" (Dubai-style) texture matrix as a primary signal of snack quality and functional efficacy.
- **Functional Indulgence:** The market has shifted from "empty calorie" treats to "Positive Nutrition," with artisanal brands offering up to 15g of protein per cookie.
- **Big Snack Modernization:** FMCG giants (Glico, Lotte) are modernizing legacy brands by integrating fiber, protein, and sugar-free claims into familiar formats.
- **Impulse Threshold:** The 50 THB mark has emerged as the definitive impulse price threshold for functional snacks in the convenience channel.
- **Study-Ready Basket:** Retail execution increasingly groups functional snacks with cognitive-positioned beverages to capture student "exam-prep" spend.

**Objective:** Map the competitive response to the chewy functional trend.

**Observations:**

- FMCG leaders (Glico, Lotte) pivoting legacy brands (Pocky, Koala's March) toward fiber and 5g+ protein payloads.
- Integration of digital engagement, such as AR packaging for student "luck charms."

**Evidence:**
- **Indulgence Leaders:** 15g Protein, Low-GI sweeteners (The Rolling Pinn).
