# DataClaw Intelligence Report #013

## Executive Summary
The regulatory landscape for kids' brain supplements in Thailand has undergone a fundamental transformation in 2026. The shift to **AI-driven automated audits** has created an environment of "Instant Enforcement" on platforms like TikTok and Shopee.

Brands must now understand the strict boundary between "Accepted Nutrition" and "Illegal Medical Claims." This report provides a roadmap for utilizing **"Safe Harbor"** wording and strategic **"Proxy Claims"** (e.g., Digital Armor) to maintain digital presence.

## 2. The Analytical Rigor: Tolerance & Overages
The Feb 2026 guidance (MOPH No. 447) has increased the technical bar for manufacturing compliance.

- **Analytical Tolerance:** Results must be **≥80% of label claim**.
- **Overage Strategy:** Top players are implementing **20-30% overages** to ensure compliance throughout the 24-month shelf life.
- **License Mandate:** Absence of the **ฆอ. number** triggers automatic digital removal by AI-crawlers (SIG-040).

## 3. Strategic Verdict / Recommendations
1. **Digital Content Audit:** implement an internal AI-style scan of all existing content using "Red Line" keywords before the Thai FDA bots detect it.
2. **Ingredient Re-Stacking:** Incorporate **"Safe Harbor"** ingredients (B12, DHA) as the primary claim-drivers to provide a legal shield.
3. **Visual Proxying:** Use metaphors for "Protection" and "Readiness" rather than "Medical Improvement" in packaging design.
