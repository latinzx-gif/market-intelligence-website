# DataClaw Intelligence Report #017

## Executive Summary
The performance metrics for the elite Thai student demographic are undergoing a physiological shift. Beyond academic grades, students are now obsessing over **"Bioharmony"**—a composite of stress recovery, heart-rate variability (HRV), and deep sleep quality tracked via wearables (Oura, Apple Watch).

This report identifies the transition from "Spiking" (high-caffeine alertness) to **"Modulating"** (Alpha-wave calibration). The **"Recovery KPI"** is now the primary driver for premium supplement consumption.

The rejection of traditional energy drinks is a direct result of the Bioharmony trend. Students have identified that high-dose caffeine spikes cortisol and lowers HRV, leading to a "Red" status on their wearables. This has created a massive opening for **"HRV-Safe"** alertness products (SIG-071).
