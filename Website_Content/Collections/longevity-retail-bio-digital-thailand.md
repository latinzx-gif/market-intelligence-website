## Market Logic

Longevity is moving from private hospital packages into retail, pharmacy, diagnostics, apps, and convenience-led formats. The strongest Thailand-specific angle is not a Western biohacking copy. It is clinical credibility plus accessible formats plus tourism and digital health infrastructure.
