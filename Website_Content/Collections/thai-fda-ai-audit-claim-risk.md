# Thai FDA AI-Audit & Claim Risk

## Market Logic

Digital enforcement changes the economics of product positioning. Brands need claim language that can survive platform crawling, Thai FDA review, consumer trust checks, and marketplace visibility rules.

## Decision Use

- Identify red-line claims before launch.
- Translate product benefits into safer functional language.
- Connect compliance with GTM and ecommerce continuity.

## Open Work

- Keep exact regulatory wording separate from strategic interpretation.
- Add a formal claim-risk matrix to the public methodology.
- Route all brain, focus, disease, immunity, sugar, and clinical language through review.
